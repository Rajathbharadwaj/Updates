I started to see some good results with more epoch.After the generator pre-training, I used to the generator to generate color photos from the black&white dataset. The generator’s job is to predict the color based on the black and white photo and then generate colorized photos. After 50 epoch training, I got my preliminary results.I downloaded some black and white photos which the model has not seen before for testing. I also added a little bit noise to give the images some kind of old photo feeling.Now I have the dataset ready, I can start training. Fastai provides very useful tool for creating GAN, combining the pre-trained generator and discriminator. I created U-Net learner for the generator and pre-trained it before the GAN.
