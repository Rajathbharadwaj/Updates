On the other hand, if the weather varies a lot, the entropy would be much larger.Now, let’s talk about Cross Entropy. On average, the station sends 4.58 bits but only 2.23 bits will be useful to the recipient. It measures the average amount of information that you get when you learn the weather each day. The weather is coded in such a way that it is unambiguous and if you chain multiple messages, there’s only one way to interpret the sequence of bits. If our predictions are perfect, that is the predicted distribution is equal to the true distribution, then the cross-entropy is simply equal to entropy.
