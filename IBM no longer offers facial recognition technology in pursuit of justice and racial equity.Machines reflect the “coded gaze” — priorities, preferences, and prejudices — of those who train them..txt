But is that enough?High-level scorecards inform casual consumers on specific state and federal agency performance, but neglect specific metrics on Racial Bias. When facial recognition programs search mugshot databases, POCs (particularly young Black men) are overrepresented in the possible matches. According to a study published by MIT and Stanford, these biases exist because algorithms are fed training data that’s more than 77% men and 83% White people. AI fueled automation helps determine who is fired, hired, promoted, granted a loan or insurance, and even how long someone spends in prison. We need to better understand how AI contributes to cycles of systemic racism and bias before distributing and profiting. Algorithms perform worse when analyzing Black and Brown bodies compared to others, and are up to 100 times more likely to misidentify POCs. Private companies are finally taking steps to prioritize people over profits.
