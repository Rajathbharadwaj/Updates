These data are used to develop machine and deep learning algorithms to automatically detect animals, vegetation, and soil-type. With WildIntelligenceLab we aim to advance the research in this field by applying modern instance segmentation models to segment the aerial imagery taken by the drone. To apply those models to the high-resolution drone images, we explore the use of different architectures and inspect their performance. The model output will allow detection of animals, vegetation, and even aardvark holes. In this project, new data acquisition techniques are being developed.
