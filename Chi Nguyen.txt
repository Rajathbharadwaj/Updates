I will use data related to Germany as an example.Exploratory Data Analysis — EDA is an indispensable step in data mining. To interpret various aspects of a data set like its distribution, principal or interference, it is necessary to visualize our data in different graphs or images. Pandas by far offers many different ways to filter your dataframes to get your selected subsets of data. When doing different kinds of analysis, you will need to collect enough data to perform your task. However, sometimes the data you need is just a part of the site. Subset selection is one of the most frequently performed steps in data manipulation. Fortunately, Python offers a lot of libraries to make visualization more convenient and easier than ever.
