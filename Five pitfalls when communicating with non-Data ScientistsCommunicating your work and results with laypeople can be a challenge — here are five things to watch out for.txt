One of the single most important aspects to a strong communication of data science results is that the reader can easily follow your logical flow. Credibility comes from clear communication of the logic of your approach.It is an extremely common behavior for data scientists to overstate their results in punchy headlines. For many data scientists, often the coding and the analytics are the easy part. Some data scientists think that showing code makes their work look credible, but that’s a mistake. Thanks to these, I now find that I can communicate much more effectively than when I first started out in data science. A quick description of the data source, providing an opportunity for someone to shout if they are concerned about it, is all that’s necessary. The challenge comes when you have to communicate the results of your work to non-data scientists.
