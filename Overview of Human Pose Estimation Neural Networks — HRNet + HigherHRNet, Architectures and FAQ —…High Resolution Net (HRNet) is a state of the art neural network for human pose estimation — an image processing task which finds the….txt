During inference both heatmaps are mean aggregated to the higher resolution and the highest valued points are chosen for keypoint detection. The trapezoid is a deconvolution layer which outputs a 2 times higher resolution with 4 residual blocks following. The first two convolutions diminish the input size according to the expected heatmap size. This scale variation does not exist in top-down methods because all person instances are normalized to the same scale. The tags are calculated according to the “Associative Embedding” method described in this paper. This is either a mistake in the code or not explicitly explained in the paper. HigherHRNet also achieves state-of-the-art results on the CrowdPose dataset.
