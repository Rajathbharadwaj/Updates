In my after work hours, I’m a YouTuber (AKA the Data Professor) making online videos about data science. Owing to the abundance of data in biology, this has incentivized my transition of becoming a data scientist. If you look into job descriptions of data scientists on LinkedIn, you will notice that there are various first degrees that data scientists may have. You might have heard from somewhere that you can learn data science in 3 months or less.But really, is that at all possible? I am still continuing to learn new concepts in data science on a daily basis. If I could turn back the clock and decide again what I would like to do I will stick to the same path and become a data scientist. This journey won’t be easy so you will have to put in the extra effort to make it work.My data science journey has been very phenomenal.
