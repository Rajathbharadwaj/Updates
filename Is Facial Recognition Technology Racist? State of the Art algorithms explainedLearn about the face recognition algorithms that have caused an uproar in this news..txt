A human can understand that if it sees another face with similar features, then there is a high chance that the new face belongs to the same person. This is analogous to an image of my face and an image of your face. Then the cropped faces are inputted the face identification model to get a feature vector. The issue is simply with the training dataset used to train the face recognition models. Face identification uses a class of networks called Siamese Networks.
