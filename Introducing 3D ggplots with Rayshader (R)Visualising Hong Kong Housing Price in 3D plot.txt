(scale_colour_gradient for geom_point, scale_fill_gradient for geom_polygon)The script to convert the 2D plot to a 3D plot is very simple. On the line, scale_fill_gradient, the polygons are shown in the map are filled by colour based on population data. Most importantly, it allows you to directly transform the ggplot2 objects into 3D plot.In this demonstration, the Hong Kong population and housing price data are used. The 3D plot is shown in the rgl window. However, remember to close the window after viewing the plot. The user can also create a cinematic depth of field post-processing effect to direct the user’s focus to important regions in the figure. Rayshader is a powerful package in R supporting 2D and 3D data visualisation.
