Data augmentation reminds me of semi-supervised learning in that you are creating new labeled data to train a model. Data augmentation is a useful tool to expand the amount of available labeled data for deep learning models. The advantages of data augmentation may be more pronounce with more than 4000 training images (24000 in the complex augmentation). First, additional labeled training data from augmentation in theory will improve your image classification model accuracy [WARNING!!! When performing any type of data augmentation, it is important to keep in mind the output of your model and if augmentation would affect the resulting classification.
