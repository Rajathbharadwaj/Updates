In this situation, the discriminator is learning not only fake from real images but also classifying each real image to its corresponding class. The GAN model now would combine the full framework, which combines the generator, the discriminator, and compile the model. The GAN model would be trained on real data and data generated by the generator. Instead of having the discriminator classify whether a given image is real or fake, the model can be turned into a multiclass classifier. This requires loss functions that balance the training on one side (discriminator) while also improving the training on the other side of (generator).
