…Between hosting the Towards Data Science podcast, and coaching data science mentees at SharpestMinds, I’ve realized that trying to become a data scientist is like drinking from two firehoses. This year reminded us repeatedly that data and the data science community are astonishingly powerful tools for understanding and improving our lives. We’ve shared thoughtful posts from authors brand new to data science and machine learning, and we’ve discussed ideas and techniques with experts across multiple fields. Hence, it is no wonder that data visualization is an essential part of data science. …When I was just getting started in data science, I never thought I would need to tackle deep learning. In its greatest moments, data visualization can summarize in a few seconds, the results of days of data analysis. Not only does it enable us to understand ourselves and the world around us, but it also helps us make better decisions.
