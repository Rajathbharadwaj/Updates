On a recent project we were called in to create a pipeline that has the ability to convert PDF documents to text. Normally, on average there would be 30–40 documents per hour, but as high as 100 during peak periods. 25 concurrently running containers then a new EKS Node is automatically provisioned by EKS. Since their business was growing the client expressed a need to OCR up to a thousand documents per day. The incoming PDF documents were typically 100 pages and could contain both typewritten and handwritten text. After the peak demands have been met a wait period kicks.
