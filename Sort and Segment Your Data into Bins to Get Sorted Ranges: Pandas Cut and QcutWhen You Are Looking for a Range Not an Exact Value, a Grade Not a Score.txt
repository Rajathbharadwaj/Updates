There are many scenarios where we need to define the bins discretely and use them in the data analysis.This function tries to divide the data into equal-sized bins. The bins are defined using percentiles, based on the distribution and not on the actual numeric edges of the bins. In the cut method, you decide the scores for a certain grade.In this example, it may look like the cut method is more appropriate. Grade the students based on the distribution.Make a list of grades that we may want to use. But if we use the cut method and pass bins=4, the bins thresholds will be 25, 50, 75, 100.
