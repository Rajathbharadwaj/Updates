In essence, the Education AI system assists teachers in managing the class and supporting all students more effectively.Hundreds of students already tried the system through their tablets. After using the system for a limited period, most of the students showed an improvement when compared to the control group (who did not use the system). The system will also provide real-time feedback that both teachers and students can act upon to improve future learning. An Education AI system would act as a repository of best practices, thus ensuring that the experience of every child is consistently high. It will also optimise our human capital by ensuring that our children get the most out of our educational system. The system handholds weaker students and helps them to excel, while also challenging the gifted ones. It will equip them with appropriate tools and statistics that will help them monitor their entire class and make them focus on those students who need their help.
