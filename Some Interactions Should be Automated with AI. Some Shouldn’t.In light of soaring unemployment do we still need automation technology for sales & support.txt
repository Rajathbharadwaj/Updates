It uses transformer networks to answer basic customer questions from website content, saving time for sales and support associates.On the surface this seems great — time is money, right? If this is a common enough issue, a custom tool could be build using something like dialogflow to answer this or many other questions. In theory, almost all support questions could be answered by a sufficiently advanced AI. Everything else is a long-tailed “hard” question that may require synthesis of multiple sources and/or knowledge that isn’t available to the customer. Sales, on the other hand, is more about building relationships than answering questions about a product or service. A world without sales teams would be a world in which people bought less, which would be a step back economically.
