So, with a wide range of plausible thoughts, what really is data science?Well firstly, data science is not about building jaw-dropping models and writing thousands of lines of code. Consider a data science hierarchy of needs for a company where data collection is at the bottom and artificial intelligence is at the top. Rather, data science is about using data to create as much impact as possible to the relevant parties. Initially, prior to popularizing data science, the term ‘data mining’ was the hot topic. Often, data scientists will be given very ambiguous and difficult problems, and it would be their task to guide the relevant parties in the right direction using data. That included but was not limited to data collection, analysis, modelling and data applications. But when persons think about data science, they immediately think about building jaw-dropping models and writing thousands of lines of code.
