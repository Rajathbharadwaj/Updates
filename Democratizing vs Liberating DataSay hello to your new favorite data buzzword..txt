So how do we create this for our modern data systems?Liberating data means not only enabling access for every member of our organization or collective. We have the platforms and means to democratize data all day long, but is that data really usable? It means giving them all the tools they need to both access and use the data.It’s not enough to give someone access to a data lake. Data lakes have managed to largely democratize data within the organization. Your org will be fighting over the handful of Data Scientists who can wrangle and make that data usable. You need to set up easy access, pipelines, tooling, and empower your workforce to get in and dig around.In Finance we have lots of issues with liberating data. Our mission should turn from democratizing data to liberating data.
