The growth rate of Interpolation Search time complexity is smaller compared to Binary Search.The best case for Interpolation Search happens when the middle (our approximation) is the desired key. In the worst-case scenario, we will need to traverse all of the elements in the array, resulting in the O(n) time complexity. This method can outrun binary search in cases where the elements of the array are uniformly distributed. Interpolation Search estimates the position of the key value in the array. This makes the best case time complexity is O(1). However, larger arrays and the ones that are uniformly distributed are Interpolation Search’s forte.
