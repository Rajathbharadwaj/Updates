In vertex colouring, we try to colour the vertices of a graph using k colours and any two adjacent vertices should not have the same colour. Similarly, vertex 6 should come after vertices 4 and 5.Graph colouring assigns colours to elements of a graph while ensuring certain conditions. If we start from one vertex, travel along a path and end up at the starting vertex, then this path is a cycle. Vertex colouring is the most commonly used graph colouring technique.
